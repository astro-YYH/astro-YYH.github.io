Research Interests

I am interested in various areas in Astrophysics, some of them are:

  • The formation and evolution of galaxies: circumgalactic medium (CGM), glactic winds (e.g., stellar winds, supernovae, AGNs, etc.), intergalactic medium (IGM)
  • The formation of seeds of (supermassive) black holes: direct-collapse black holes
  • The nature of dark matter: dark matter detection; how about MOND?
  • Some ones in cosmology

Current/Previous Work

I am currently working on topics related to galaxies. Specifically, my research focuses on tubulent mixing layers (TMLs) arising from shear flows in the context of Astronomy (e.g., galactic winds and cold gas clouds, high-velocity clouds and hot haloes of galaxies), which is an attractive explanation of the ubiquity of some ions (most notably, O VI) detected in absorption in quasar sightlines intersecting the CGM.
